# Services

## Lump Sum — $3,000

A one-time investment. I design and build your site from scratch — you own it outright. Up to 5 pages included; additional pages at $100 each. A blog can be added for $250.

After launch:
- **Hosting** — $25/mo (required)
- **Maintenance** — $100/mo (optional — up to 5 hrs/mo of edits, updates, upkeep)
- **Hourly** — $75/hr for one-off work outside scope (new pages, features, etc.)

## Monthly — $500 setup + $200/mo

No large upfront cost. I design and build your site and bundle everything — hosting, edits, and support — into one predictable monthly payment. Requires a 12-month minimum. Up to 5 pages included; additional pages at $100 each. A blog can be added for $250.

After 12 months, the price drops to $100/mo — hosting and maintenance bundled, month-to-month.

## Maintenance — $100/mo

Up to 5 hours of edits per month, security patches, hosting, and general upkeep — all handled. Unused hours don't roll over. Available as a standalone plan for existing sites (requires a brief review before onboarding) or as the natural continuation of the Monthly plan after month 12.

## Hourly — $75/hr

For one-off work that doesn't fit a retainer. New pages, small features, consults, or anything outside the scope of an existing plan.
