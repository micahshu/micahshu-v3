When you define every color, every spacing step, every type size as a named token, something unexpected happens: the system starts to feel like a point of view.

A token isn't just a value. It's a decision — a commitment to a particular shade of grey, a particular rhythm of space. Stack enough of them and you've built a palette that is distinctly yours.
